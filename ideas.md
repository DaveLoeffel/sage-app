## Executive Summary

Sage is a personalized AI executive assistant that provides Dave Loeffel with 100% context persistence, intelligent follow-up tracking, and unified data synthesis across all professional and personal domains. The system eliminates dropped follow-ups, reduces cognitive load, and provides proactive intelligence through daily briefings and weekly reviews.

**Primary Success Metric (30-Day):** Bulletproof follow-up tracking—no email falls through the cracks.

**Core Principles:**
1. **100% Context** – Unified knowledge across email, meetings, documents, and personal life
2. **100% Persistence** – Nothing forgotten between sessions
3. **100% Accuracy** – Multi-source verification; conflicts surfaced for human decision
4. **Human-in-the-Loop** – Draft only, never send; recommend but never authorize spending

## Core Functionality

### 1. Context Persistence
- Remembers all interactions across sessions
- Maintains unified knowledge base of contacts, properties, family, and business
- No need to re-explain context in new sessions

### 2. Follow-Up Management
- Automatic tracking of emails requiring follow-up
- Deadline monitoring
- Escalation protocols (Day 2 reminder, Day 7 supervisor CC)
- Auto-closure when replies received
- Prevents dropped communications

### 3. Email Intelligence
- Categorizes incoming email by priority
- Identifies action items
- Suggests draft responses
- Learns communication style from sent folder
- Never sends without approval

### 4. Meeting Support
- Pre-meeting context preparation
- Integration of previous meeting notes
- Post-meeting action item extraction
- Relationship history tracking

### 5. Calendar Management
- Multi-calendar integration (Dave + Laura)
- Conflict detection
- Event context enrichment
- Family/business schedule coordination

### 6. Information Synthesis
- Combines data from email, calendar, documents, meetings
- Identifies patterns and connections
- Surfaces relevant information proactively
- Multi-source verification before presenting information

### 7. Proactive Intelligence
- Daily briefings with curated information
- Weekly reviews and planning
- Deadline monitoring
- News and market updates relevant to business/interests

### 8. Voice & Style Matching
- Learns from Dave's sent emails
- Maintains consistent tone across recipients
- Formal but approachable
- No templates or catchphrases

## Safety Protocols

### Human-in-the-Loop Requirements
- **Never send emails automatically** – All emails are drafts for approval
- **Never authorize spending** – Financial recommendations only, no transactions
- **Never delete or archive without permission**
- **Never make commitments on Dave's behalf**

### Accuracy & Verification
- Multi-source verification for factual information
- Surface conflicts or inconsistencies for human decision
- Explicit confidence levels when uncertain
- Cite sources for key information

### Privacy & Security
- No sharing of personal or business data externally
- Secure handling of credentials and API keys
- Family information treated with highest confidentiality
- Investor communications handled with appropriate discretion
